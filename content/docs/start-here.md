# Welcome to Zenvi

We are two founders building a video editor that keeps your footage on your machine and uses AI where it actually helps. No upload queue, no mystery cloud. When something breaks or feels slow, we get it. This guide exists so you can move fast without guessing.

## What Zenvi does

Zenvi combines a classic timeline (tracks, clips, transitions, export) with an **assistant** that can run editing actions for you: import media, split clips, add transitions, generate short videos, search scenes, and more. Heavy work still takes time on your hardware or over the APIs you configure, but the loop is meant to feel immediate.
